Examination Rules & Instructions
  1. Total Exam Duration: You have exactly 2 hours (120 minutes) to answer 180 questions across your four registered subjects.
  2. Subject Navigation: Use the subject tabs at the top of the interface to switch between your subjects. You can attempt subjects in any order.
  3. Question Navigation: Use NEXT and PREVIOUS buttons to move between questions. You can also use the Question Palette on the right to jump directly to any question number.
  4. Selecting Answers: Click on your preferred option (A, B, C, or D). Your selection is saved automatically.
  5. Reviewing Questions: If you are unsure about an answer, click MARK FOR REVIEW. The question number in the palette will turn ORANGE.
  6. Palette Indicators:
    • GREY: Not yet attempted.
    • GREEN: Answered and saved.
    • ORANGE: Marked for Review.
    • GOLD BORDER: Current active question.
  7. Automatic Submission: The exam will submit itself once the timer reaches 00:00:00. Do not wait until the last minute before clicking final submit.
  8. Malpractice: Any attempt to use external aids, communicate with other candidates, or bypass security features will result in immediate disqualification.
